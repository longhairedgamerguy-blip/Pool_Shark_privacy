Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Purrr ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Purrr does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. The App does not require you to create an account or provide any personal details to use it.

Your in-app settings — such as your saved sound mix, sleep-timer choice, haptic preference, and sound-tuning adjustments — are stored only locally on your device so the App can restore them the next time you open it. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Advertising

The App may display advertisements served by Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect certain device information and use cookies or similar technologies to serve relevant ads. This data collection is governed by Google's own privacy policy. You can review Google's privacy practices at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

To opt out of interest-based advertising by Google, you can visit https://www.google.com/settings/ads or adjust your device's ad settings.

Device Permissions

The App may use your device's vibration feature to provide an optional "haptic purr" that you can feel while a sound is playing. This is used purely to produce vibration on your device; it does not collect or transmit any information.

The App plays audio in the background and may keep an audio session or media notification active while a sound is playing, so playback can continue when the App is minimized or your screen is locked.

Third-Party Services

Other than Google AdMob, the App does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or data-sharing services.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
